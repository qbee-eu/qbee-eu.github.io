Yasaman Samadi is PhD Student at University of Porto and researcher at QBee.eu working on the Quantum Micro-Architecture. She received her Bachelor of Science in Hardware Engineering at Sheikh Bahaei University in Iran. She received her Master of Science in Computer Architecture Engineering at Islamic Azad University, Science and Research Branches of Tehran in Iran, in 2019. Her research interests lie on low level control electronic systems, FPGAs, and Micro-Architecture for Quantum Accelerators.

Exploring Quantum Computer Micro-Architectures.
